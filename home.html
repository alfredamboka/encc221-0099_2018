Web Application for Analyzing Diabesity prevalence and Risk Factors

Project Overview

Diabesity prevalence have been on the rise since 1980 which results to a new health crisis of 21st century. Due to diverse health complication associated with the disease, the world face global burden economically and on quality of life. Existing studies provides possible solution on risk factors Identification using machine and deep learning. Visualization is done though is limited to either prevalence or risk factors.
Proposed system bridge the gap by providing capabilities for both prevalence and associated risk factors. The solution allows for local coverage as compared to global/national level. The system is limited by accuracies and primary data integration implementation protocols.
